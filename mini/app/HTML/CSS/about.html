About Us

Our project objective is to assist the government in developing effective and humane crime prevention strategies and in establishing and maintaining institutional frameworks for their implementation and review so as to achieve safety for individuals in society.

This is done by analyzing and anticipating crime hotspots, visualizing data on maps and generating statistical reports to monitor the progress of institutions responsible for crime prevention.
